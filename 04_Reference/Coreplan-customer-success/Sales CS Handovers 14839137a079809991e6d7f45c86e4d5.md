# **Desktop Study**

## What is it?

It is a demonstration of the CorePlan platform to the customer with the customerâ€™s data. The demonstration is to address key questions the customer has or showcase the capabilities of key use cases the customer is experiencing pain. This is uncovered through the sales process through `deep discovery questions` (link).

The desktop study also acts as a *trojan horse* to identify gaps in the customers reporting or dataset. We have in instances found that customers are spending exorbitant amounts in excess (mining) or losing revenue (drilling).

## What is needed for a desktop study?

- Drilling Plod Data in excel format for a period of at least 2 billing cycles but max 4.
- Invoice/claims for the corresponding period
- schedule of rates for that plod data
- maximum 5 rigs and 1 project only (unless approved by manager)

## Acceptance criteria for a desktop study

Running a desktop study is a large investment and cost from our side. *(Most traditional consultancies or software companies charge big bucks for this type of studies).* So ensuring that the you the customer and the cs team are setup for success means that the key qualification criteria need to be met for a customer to be accepted by CS to do a desktop study and manager approval.

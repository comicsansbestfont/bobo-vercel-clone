# Business Case

### What is it?

It's a tailored presentation or document to help prospects prepare an internal proposal for decision-makers. This document outlines the prospectâ€™s organisationâ€™s SPICED and shows how CorePlan can add value. It highlights the current situation, the problem, its impact, and the negative consequences if not addressed by a specific deadline. Then, it presents how CorePlan can solve these issues and help decision-makers achieve their success metrics.

### Why?

When you discover that the person(s) youâ€™re demoing to aren't the decision-makers, our mission is to empower them to sell CorePlan to their team.

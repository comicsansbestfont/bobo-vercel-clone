The highlevel is to go from 

1. Showcase the project page walk through from top to bottom
2. Runthrough of the drill hole data model (depending on audeince)
3. showing them how to import drill holes, assoicate them to drill programs and track program costs
4. drill plod review, approve and touch on the contracts and daily plod cost tracking 
5. overview of the contract
6. progress claim for end of month for cost reconciliation
7. other reports on project level, global dashboards and custom reporting.

# [EXP] SPICED Qualification Structure

| **FRAMEWORK** | **POSSIBLE QUESTIONS** |
| --- | --- |
| **SITUATION** | **HIGH LEVEL**
*How are you lot managing your drill programs today?

Are you currently using excel or your own software to manage your drill programs?

How many metres are you drilling today?

How many rigs do you have active today?* 

**DETAILS OF THE WORKFLOW**
*Could you walk me, step-by-step, through what your typical workflow looks like?

After mapping your holes, how are you working with all that information?

How are you receiving your plods from your drill contractors today?

I can imagine it’s really tedious managing your drilling with spreadsheets/legacy software, could you give me an idea of how long it takes to complete [insert part of workflow you’re after e.g. invoice recon, keying data into their system, etc]*

**PERSONA BASED**
*Who from your team is involved in the process? 

Could you walk me through who does what role in this whole workflow?* |
| **PAIN** | *With the way things are now, what is pushing you to look for a change?

I’ve heard it from other companies, but I want to understand from your perspective what is so painful about using spreadsheets/legacy software to push to to look for a change?*

**GUIDED QUESTION**
*I typically hear companies using spreadsheets/legacy software [insert below], do you see that happening with you today?
- do a lot of double handling 
- see a lot of errors 
- can’t keep track of their costs vs budget*  |
| **IMPACT** | *Now that we understand what issues you’re facing today, how would you like CorePlan to help with this? 

What do you think is the greatest ROI CorePlan can deliver given your current pains from today?

If you were to digitise your workflow and save all these hours, how do you think the business can benefit from all this?* 

**GUIDED QUESTION
***It sounds like [insert pain], and CorePlan can help with that. Am I right to say the end goal is to [insert impact]?* |
| **CRITICAL EVENT**  | *What pushed you to look into CorePlan? 

Is there an important event coming up which led you to explore CorePlan?* |
| **DECISION** | *Who in your team and company will be involved in the decision making process?

So let’s say you’re happy with CorePlan and all goes well, who needs to sign off on this?

When you’re buying a software like this, how does the process typically work?* |

**10 Questions To Ask Before Writing (Dan Kennedy)**

1. What keeps them awake at night?
2. What are they afraid of?
3. What are they angry about?
4. What are their top 3 daily frustrations?
5. What trends are occurring in their business/life?
6. What do they secretly admire the most?
7. Is there a built in bias to how they make decisions? (ex: engineers = analytical)
8. Who is selling something similar to their product and how? (what can you learn from them)
9. Do they have their own language? (terms they use)
10. Who has tried selling something similar and why did that effort fail?

**Steve Jobs Rule Of 3**
Stick to the rule of threes. Always. When presenting options. 1-2-3. 3 secrets. 3 key points. 3 things, three goals.

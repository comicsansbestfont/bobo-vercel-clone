The brand messaging and your sales messaging are the 2 key pillars of your
company communication.

Your brand messaging is your unique view and
[positioning](https://www.mrrunlocked.com/p/saas-positioning-6-different-ways)
of:

  * how do you see the market on a higher level (big picture)?

  * why do you want to serve the market (your motivation)?

  * why now? What are macro trends in our society supporting your business?

Talking about the purpose of the company (The WHY) and passion for the topic
of your business are effective pr strategies. A great starting point for
powerful brand messaging can be [the Golden Circle (WHY/HOW/WHAT) of Simon
Sinek.](https://simonsinek.com/commit/the-golden-circle)

There are 2 main sources on how to get referrals - number 1 is asking your

Not asking your existing customers for referrals is [one of the most common

Whatâ€™s important about a referral marketing program:

  * referral incentive: What is the referral bonus (cash vs. free product usage vs. other perks) and who gets the bonus when?

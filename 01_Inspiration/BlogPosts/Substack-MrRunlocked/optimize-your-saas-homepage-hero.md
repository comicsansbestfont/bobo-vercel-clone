Your above-the-fold/hero section needs to grab the attention.

But more importantly to give answers to the most important questions visitors
have.

Most visitors decide in a few seconds if they bounce or stay and read the rest
of your homepage.

So if your hero section leaves them with many questions, you will probably
lose them.

1ï¸âƒ£ **Product Category**

3ï¸âƒ£ **Benefits**

What is the value of the product? What results can I expect?

4ï¸âƒ£ **ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)**

5ï¸âƒ£ **Product video/animation**

6ï¸âƒ£**Social proof/trust**

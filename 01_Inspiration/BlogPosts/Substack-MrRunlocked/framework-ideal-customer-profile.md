**Tip** : In a competitive space, consider developing a disruptive pricing
model to establish a leading position in the market.

Both of these models had their shortcomings, as they charged customers even
when the app didn't bring them any value, such as when customers didn't sell
any pre-orders.

We adopted a different approach by billing customers based on the number of
pre-orders they sold each month (â€”> awesome example for [value-based
pricing](https://startupbusinesstips.substack.com/p/the-ultimate-b2b-saas-

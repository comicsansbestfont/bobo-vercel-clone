We all agree that prospecting to referred leads (aka. warm leads) is easier
and more effective than cold prospecting.

‚ÄòWarm‚Äô leads have a higher trust in you and therefore show higher win rates.

A great opportunity to get these ‚Äòwarm intros‚Äô are all your interactions with

The goal of referral sales is getting your existing customers (and prospects)

relevant leads in their network, plus using their names when contacting the
new leads.

But if you are successful in getting referrals and intros to other relevant
leads depends a lot on HOW you ask them.

That‚Äôs why I‚Äôve created some blurbs that you can use straight away.

Here are 3 ways how to get more intros/referrals:

> customer-profile)) and what our product can do for them, who are 2-3 other
> companies/people you think can benefit from it?_‚Äù

> ‚Äú _Thanks. Would you mind making an email intro for me? If you want, I can

Being successful with Content Marketing consists of 5 steps:

  1. Content Research

  2. Content Planning

  3. Content Creation

  4. Content Distribution

  5. Content Optimization (for SEO)

Most early-stage founders know the importance of Content Marketing as one (or
primary) growth channel in the early days. Creating valuable content creates

But here‚Äôs the thing: It‚Äôs not enough to ‚Äòjust‚Äô create content, publish (the

Distribution of the content is neglected by most founders. I would even say

begin with the first 4) steps.

It all starts with getting to know your ICP (if you don‚Äôt know what this is,

competitors](https://startupbusinesstips.substack.com/p/3-types-of-
competition) check out their content strategy.

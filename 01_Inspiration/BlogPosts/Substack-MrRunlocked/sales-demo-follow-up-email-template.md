> Horizontal SaaS products cannot be marketed horizontally. Instead, vertical
> personas, use cases, or industries are required to be effective.

The biggest mistake you can make as a marketer for a horizontal product is
going too broad. Broad shows up in two ways:

**1/ Broad in messaging** In this scenario, you‚Äôre using a wider message to
try and teach a large audience. And it won't work because it will lack the
clarity for an individual to engage.

**2/ Broad in resourcing** In this scenario, you are using targeted messaging,
which is great. But you are doing it with too many different audiences at
once. This doesn't work because in your early stage you will have limited
resources to put together the marketing materials needed to support these
different segments.

**The solution to this challenge?**

  1. Experiment to find a targeted segment that aligns the best with your product

  2. Double down with a focus on that segment until you have made the marketing for that segment 100% operational

  3. Move on sequentially to the next segment until you have covered them all

If you want to learn more about how to combat this horizontal marketing
challenge, you can read more about Horizontal GTM phases

When you‚Äôre just starting, your product is usually focused. It does one or two

When it comes to [GTM assets](https://www.mrrunlocked.com/p/saas-gtm-

Quick reminder, a good homepage should answer these 6 key questions for your
visitors:

  1. What is the product/service they offer?

  2. What problem does it solve for me?

  3. What can I do with the product?

  4. Who is it for? Is this product for me?

  5. What are the main benefits for me?

  6. Can I trust the company?

I recommend that your homepage come with the following sections:

  1. (Sticky) Navigation bar

  2. Hero Section

  3. Social Proof

  4. Before X (Problem / Status Quo)

  5. Intro Solution

  6. Capabilities Section

  7. Special Purpose Sections

  8. Main CTA

‚ùå NOT a page purely on 1 specific use case or 1 specific feature.

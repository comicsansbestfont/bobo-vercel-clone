You have lots of traffic, but low sign-up conversion rates?

A [powerful](https://www.mrrunlocked.com/p/saas-homepage-copywriting-guide)

Visitors immediately need to know how your product helps them.

And the top section (called the above-the-fold section) receives the most
attention from your visitors because it's the first thing they see before
scrolling down.

This section needs to grab the attention of the visitor - otherwise, you will
lose them!

**Revamp the top section of your website so it answers the two main

  1. How does your product help the visitor? (Main Benefit, Main Job to be done)

Don't do what most salespeople do when they start in Sales. ‚ùå

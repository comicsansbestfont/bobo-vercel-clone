## 5b. Ownership Sanity Check -- NEW (v2.2)

*This is a "bounding box" check - compares framework output against market-implied pre-money based on round size and dilution.*

### Talvin's Stated Raise

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Founder's Stated Raise** | $1,000,000 USD |
| **Current Committed** | $200,000 USD |
| **Stated Valuation** | $10,000,000 pre-money |
| **Implied Dilution** | 9.1% ($1M / $11M post) |

### Market Norms Check (AU/NZ 2024-25)

| Round Type | Typical Raise | Typical Dilution | Implied Pre-Money Range |
|------------|---------------|------------------|-------------------------|
| Pre-seed (angel-only) | $250K-$750K | 5%-12% | $1.8M - $14.75M |
| **Pre-seed (with lead)** | **$750K-$2M** | **10%-20%** | **$3.0M - $18M** |
| Seed | $2M-$6M | 15%-25% | $6M - $34M |

**Talvin's Round Classification:** Pre-seed with lead (seeking $1M with lead investor)

### Implied Pre-Money Calculation

Based on $1M raise at various dilution levels:

| Dilution | Formula | Implied Pre-Money |
|----------|---------|-------------------|
| 10% | ($1M ÷ 0.10) - $1M | **$9.0M** |
| 15% | ($1M ÷ 0.15) - $1M | **$5.67M** |
| 20% | ($1M ÷ 0.20) - $1M | **$4.0M** |
| 25% | ($1M ÷ 0.25) - $1M | **$3.0M** |

**Market-Implied Range for $1M Raise:** $3.0M - $9.0M pre-money (at 10-25% dilution)

### Alignment Assessment

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Framework Valuation Range** | $1.7M - $2.4M |
| **Market-Implied Pre-Money (typical dilution)** | $3.0M - $9.0M |
| **Founder's Expectation** | $10.0M |
| **Alignment Status** | **Gap** |

**Analysis:**

1. **Framework vs Market-Implied Gap:** The framework range ($1.7M-$2.4M) is below the lower bound of market-implied pre-money for a $1M raise at typical dilution ($3.0M). This suggests either:
   - The raise amount is too large for the stage (should raise less)
   - Investors will expect higher dilution (25-35%) if they invest at framework valuation
   - Founder expectations need significant calibration

2. **Founder Expectation vs Framework:** Founder expects $10M, framework suggests $1.7M-$2.4M - a 320% gap.

3. **Founder Expectation vs Market-Implied:** $10M is at the upper edge of market-implied for a $1M pre-seed, but only at 9.1% dilution. This is aggressive for Phase 1a with $12K ARR.

4. **Realistic Scenario:** If an investor agreed to the framework valuation range:
   - At $2.0M pre-money + $1M raise = $3M post → **33% dilution**
   - This is above typical pre-seed dilution (10-20%) but reasonable for Phase 1a

**Sanity Check Conclusion:**
The $1M raise at $10M pre-money is misaligned with both the framework valuation AND market norms for Phase 1a companies. A more realistic scenario:
- **Option A:** Raise $500K at $2M pre (20% dilution) - aligns with framework and market
- **Option B:** Raise $1M at $3M pre (25% dilution) - stretches framework but within market range
- **Current ask:** Raise $1M at $10M pre (9.1% dilution) - misaligned, will struggle to close
